About Fruit & Vegetable Identifier

This web application helps you identify various fruits and vegetables by analyzing images you upload. It provides detailed information such as the name, nutritional benefits, and freshness status (fresh or rotten) of the produce.

Our goal is to make it easy for users to quickly recognize different types of fruits and vegetables, promote healthy eating habits, and reduce food waste by identifying spoilage.

Whether you are a consumer, vendor, or just curious, this tool is designed to assist you in making informed decisions about your food.
